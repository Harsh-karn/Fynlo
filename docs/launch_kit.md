### 1. Maker's Comment (The First Comment)
*This is the comment you post immediately after the product goes live on PH.*

"Hey Product Hunt! 👋 I’m Harsh, the maker of Fynlo.

I was exhausted by the current state of Indian finance apps. The 'legacy' trackers are bloated with 'Buy Now, Pay Later' loan ads, and the newer ones require you to hand over your bank login via Account Aggregators or charge exorbitant subscription fees.

So, I built **Fynlo**. 
It’s a pure, privacy-first expense tracker powered by a localized Machine Learning engine.

**Here’s what makes Fynlo different:**
1. **Zero Data Sharing:** We use a lightweight, on-server Scikit-Learn ML pipeline to categorize your transactions. We do NOT send your financial data to OpenAI or Gemini.
2. **No Bloatware:** No loan selling. No credit card upsells. Just clean, actionable financial intelligence.
3. **True Privacy:** We are strictly compliant with India’s DPDP Act. You own your data, and you can nuke your account with one click if you choose to.
4. **Platform Agnostic:** We parse SMS natively on Android and support PDF statement uploads on the web. 

Fynlo is free to start. For power users, our Pro tier unlocks advanced PDF parsing and unlimited AI categorization for just ₹299/month. 

I’d love to hear your feedback on the UI and the accuracy of our ML categorization! I'll be hanging out in the comments all day to answer your questions. 🚀"
